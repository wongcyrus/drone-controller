Controls

Keyboard (Single Mode)

  • W/A/S/D: Move Forward/Left/Back/Right
  • Q/E: Move Up/Down
  • R/T: Rotate Left/Right
  • Space: Takeoff/Land

Mouse Controls

  • Left Click + Drag: Rotate camera
  • Right Click + Drag: Pan camera
  • Scroll: Zoom in/out
  • Click Drone: Select for control
